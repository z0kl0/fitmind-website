IT ALL STARTED
WITH A SHIFT

FitMind was founded in 2019 by Chantal, inspired by her experience in the corporate world and her deep belief that real change starts in the mind. After witnessing how poor communication, lack of accountability, and external validation limited both individuals and organizations, she began exploring the connection between mindset, performance, and self esteem.

Drawing from her background in psychology, fitness, leadership, and life coaching, Chantal created FitMind to integrate physical, mental, and emotional growth into one aligned approach. Leaving a 15 year executive career in banking to follow this vision, she built FitMind around a simple guiding principle: Shape your mind, Change your life.
